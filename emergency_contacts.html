Emergency Procedures

What to do in case of an emergency

Call for Help

Dial the appropriate emergency number based on the situation. Stay calm and provide clear information about your location and the emergency.

Provide Location Details

Clearly state your exact location on campus, including building name, floor, and room number if possible.

Alert Others

If safe to do so, alert people nearby about the emergency and follow established evacuation procedures.

First Aid

If trained, provide basic first aid while waiting for professional medical help to arrive.
